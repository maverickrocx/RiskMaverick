Counterparty Credit Risk (CCR)

Framework

The risk that a trading counterparty defaults before settling its obligations — quantified through exposure, default probability and loss severity.

EAD
Exposure at Default
PFE
Potential Future Exposure
CVA
Credit Valuation Adjustment
PD · LGD
Default × Loss Severity

Expected loss on a derivative book is EL = EAD × PD × LGD, with SA-CCR governing regulatory exposure and CVA capital charges under Basel.
